Project & Passion Driven Development

Here at Miracle Factory, we are pushing the idea of project driven development. Every project is the result of a passion and an idea. Apart from following a simple Code of Conduct, the community has no restriction on what is being built, the developers are only limited by their own imagination. We are trying to build an open, enthusiastic and equal atmosphere where everyone can create their own project and are welcomed to contribute to other projects.


Building with passion and love.

Love + AI + Team = Power AI

We love developing AI systems, it's a part of who we are. Through working together as a team, we were able to create some amazing AI projects, taking AI systems into the undiscovered countries. Such efforts delivered projects like Submarine HSAOIS, a powerful and flexible motherboard production defect detection and analysis system.

Inspired by the GitHub Campus Expert project, we are also trying to gather a community of campus and academic leaders who can support the university communities and their students. We are also trying to provide a platform for Campus Experts to share their knowledge and experience with other students through Campus Expert Seminars.
